Number Guessing Game

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game! Try to guess the number between 1 and 100. You have 10 attempts to guess the correct number. Good luck!

To play the game, simply enter your guess in the input field and click the "Submit" button. The game will provide feedback on whether your guess is too low, too high, or correct. If you guess the number correctly within 10 attempts, you win! If you run out of attempts, the game will reveal the correct number. Have fun playing!
